Personal Biography

A more personal look at the motives behind the man

Hello, my name is Alex Gold. I went to UConn Hartford for 2 semesters trying to figure out what I wanted to do with my life. I almost changed colleges to the Central Connecticut State University until I discovered this bootcamp. Given the new social condtions we now face, I thought it would be the perfect time to learn how to code so that I may obtain a flexible skill that I can use for plenty of job opportunities from the safety of my own home. I also hope to use the knowledge I gain from this course to fuel my own ambitions to hopefully create something of my own or be a part of a development team that can create something unique like a unique phone application or a breathtaking video game. I want to contiue using what I learn in the bootcamp to the point where I not only understand how to be more efficient at my career but also so I can code and create on my own as well.
